Waan Afaan Oromo

Namni milyoona afurtama Afaan Oromo (“the Oromo language” afaan-ingilizi keesa) dubatan. Horn of Africa keesa, Oromon afaan ka namni hedduu dubbatu gaaru namni alaa wan Afaan Oromo hin beekani. Waaga baayyee, afaan kenya Ethiopia keesa dubbachu hin dandenee. Namni Oromo baayyee Ethiopia keesati jirran, garu kabaja nuttin keeninee. Waggaa 1991 keesati Afaan Oromo mana-barnootaa keesati barsiisu danda’ee. Waagaf baaye mootumma ka Ethiopia aadaa Oromo baleesuu fedhan gaaru assuma jirra. Mana-barnoota keesati dubbatu danda’ani, Ethiopia keesati hassa’u danda’ani, gaaru mootumman afaan offisshiyala hin gonee. Namni Oromo Oromia erra dhufnaan. Amma, Oromiyyan Ethiopia keesa jirra. Billussuumaf lolle fi billlssumaf fenee. Namni Oromo baayyee nama Ethiopia offin wamanii.
