Robyn Rihanna Fenty

  • Date of Birth: February 20th, 1988
  • Occupations: Singer, songwriter, actress, businesswoman, Ambassador of Barbados
  • Height: 5'8"
  • Net Worth: $260 Million

Rihanna was born in and grew up in Saint Michael Parish, Barbados. At a young age she showed a strong interest in singing and becoming an artist. When she was only 15, she met a music producer who saw her talent and introduced her to the world. Today, she is more than just a Grammy (and other) award winning singer/songwriter. She is a businesswoman running fashion and makeup lines, such as Fenty Beauty and Savage X Fenty. She has starred in multiple movies and was even chosen to become the Ambassador of Barbados in 2018. Robyn Rihanna Fenty is an iconic woman who continues to bring her talent to multiple fields.
